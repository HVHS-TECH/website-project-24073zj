The First Fighter Jet

The first ever fighter jet to fly was the He 178, an experimentsl german fighter powered by a copy of an english engine. On August 27, 1939, took off and circled it's factory using only the power of it's jet of it's two jet engines. This plane was never used in combat though, so the first operational jet fighter was actually the German Me-262. Soon after the Me-262 was put into service, Britan answered with it's first jet, the Gloster Meteor.

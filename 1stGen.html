Today our skies are home to some of humanities most advanced machines. capable of steath, speed, and agility to outpreform and destroy their adversaries. They feature advanced radar, extensive computer sytems, and long range missiles.

Where it All Began

The first fighter jet took flight on August 27, 1939. The experimental He 178 circled the factory only using the power of it's two jet engines. This plane was never used in combat though, so the first operational jet fighter was actually the Me262.

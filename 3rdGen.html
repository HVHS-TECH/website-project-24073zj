Fighter Jets

The Multi-Role Era

A Shift in Focus

The third generation is when fighter jets started to transition from aircraft only built for one purpose, like interceptors and air superiority jets, to multi role jets capable of multipe roles like air superioity and ground attack. Some notable fighters in this era are the f-4 phantom, the Mig-23, and the Dassult Mirage III.

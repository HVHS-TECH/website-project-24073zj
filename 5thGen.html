Fighter Jets in the Modern Era

The fighter jets of today are masterpieces of engineering. They can fly faster and higher than ever before, but also can be stealthy and precise. They feature advanced avionics, and beyond visual range missiles that track their targets even beyond the horizon.

VTOL Capabilities and Supermaneuverability

Fighter jets of today are designed to be more maneuverable than ever. Fighters like the F-35b Lightning have STOVL capability, meaning they can take off from short distances and land vertically, without needing a runway. This makes them able to operate from aircraft carriers without much runway. Another way modern fighters are made to be maneuverable is shown in supermaneuverability, where planes like the su35 and f22 can complete incredible maneuvers to evade their enemies. They use thrust vectoring, which is where special flaps are used to direct the engine's exhaust in a desired direction to help the aircraft move without relying on airflow alone.
